# World's Coolest Zoo (Fun Project)

## The World's Coolest Zoo brings you the coolest exotic fictional animals to ever exist.

Our mission at the World's Coolest Zoo is To Protect, To Preserve, To Promote. We help serve endangered fictional species 
in helping them experience the joy of life and the love they deserve. 

* Gain insight about their characteristics.
* Observe their looks
* See where they live and what they like to eat
* Show your support by sharing snapshots to friends.
